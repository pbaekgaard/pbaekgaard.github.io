Privacy Policy for AAU Grades

1. Secure Storage

AAU Grades utilizes FlutterSecureStorage to securely store certain user information, such as usernames and passwords, exclusively on the user's device. This storage mechanism is designed to provide an additional layer of security to protect sensitive data.

2. No External Transfer

We do not transfer this locally stored data to our servers or any external locations. All information remains on the user's device and is accessible only by the app.

3. Encryption and Security Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption and other security measures to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of the information stored using FlutterSecureStorage.

4. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their login credentials. We recommend using strong and unique passwords to enhance the protection of personal information stored on their devices.

5. No External Access

We do not have access to the data stored using FlutterSecureStorage. It is entirely under the user's control, and the app interacts with this information solely for authentication purposes.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of any updates, and their continued use of the AAU Grades app constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
